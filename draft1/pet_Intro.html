Story

Meet "Kiki," our adorable and loving Golden Doodle who is eagerly waiting to find a forever home! Kiki is a playful and energetic 2-year-old who will instantly steal your heart with his endearing eyes and joyful spirit. As a Golden Doodle, he's a perfect blend of the intelligence and loyalty of a Golden Retriever and the low-shedding coat and delightful charm of a Poodle. Kiki has a beautiful, soft, wavy coat that is golden in color, and his hypoallergenic nature makes him an ideal companion for those with allergies. He loves going for walks, exploring the outdoors, and playing fetch with his favorite toys. Kiki is well-socialized and gets along with other dogs, making him a wonderful addition to a household with other furry friends. This affectionate pup is also great with children and enjoys being a part of a family. Kiki has been house-trained, neutered, and is up-to-date on all his vaccinations. He's a quick learner, and with a little guidance, he'll be the perfect companion for any loving home. If you're looking to bring a bundle of joy, love, and laughter into your life, Kiki is the ideal Golden Doodle for you. He is waiting patiently to become a cherished member of his new forever family.
